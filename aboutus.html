About Barangay Ayusan 1 Tiaong Quezon

Barangay Ayusan 1 is a vibrant community nestled in Tiaong, Quezon. Established as one of the barangays in Tiaong, Ayusan 1 is home to over 2,700 residents. Our barangay has a rich history rooted in agricultural practices, local traditions, and community spirit.
