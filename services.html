Services We Offer at Barangay Ayusan 1

Our barangay offers a variety of essential services, including Health and Sanitation, where we provide free medical consultations and support through Barangay Health Workers. This service includes immunizations, maternal and child health support, and health education to promote wellness within the community.

Our Educational Support initiatives empower youth and adults alike. We offer scholarships for deserving students and Adult Literacy Classes to enhance reading, writing, and numeracy skills. Our Tutorial Services provide additional support to students in need.

We provide Social Welfare and Services for our most vulnerable residents, including pensions for senior citizens and community outreach programs. Additionally, Livelihood Training equips residents with skills for small business or employment opportunities.

Safety and Security is a top priority, with our Barangay Security Patrol ensuring peace within the community. Our Fire Safety and Disaster Preparedness initiatives involve training and drills, and our Neighborhood Watch Program encourages community vigilance.

We foster Community Engagement and Recreation through sports programs, cultural festivals, and accessible resources like the Barangay Library and Learning Centers.

Our Environmental Programs aim to protect and sustain our surroundings. We promote Tree Planting and encourage eco-friendly waste practices, including recycling and composting. Water management initiatives ensure clean and sufficient water for the community.

These services reflect our dedication to building a better, more progressive, and inclusive community at Barangay Ayusan 1.
